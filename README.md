# AI & ML Fundamentals

## Project Overview

This project explores the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). It explains the differences between AI and ML, discusses the major types of machine learning, analyzes a real-world healthcare use case, and highlights the importance of AI ethics and responsible development.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are transforming industries by improving efficiency, automation, and decision-making. Understanding their fundamentals and ethical implications is essential for future development.
